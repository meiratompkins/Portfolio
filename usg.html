I started my role as graphic designer for USG in the fall 2020 semester. As graphic designer, I generally work in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator creating social media graphics and posts. Additionally I do create promotional items and other informational graphics.

This pamphlet called "USG At a Glance" was one of the first things I started working on as a graphic designer. Everything from the layout to the graphics I created using the USG style guide and their official fonts and colors.
