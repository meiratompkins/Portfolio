About Me

Meira has lent her talent to magazine layouts, social media campaigns, and countless websites, but her best work is guided by a belief that good design transcends pixels and code. Without a strong connection to the client and their mission, even the most beautiful end result would have no impact. In this regard, Meira’s empathy and positivity have been as instrumental to her role at Scout as her artistic ability. She blends both to tell the client’s story in a visually compelling way, taking pride in the entire process from ideation to go-live.
