Project Overview

The Peggy Reid Ogden Artwork website is an exceptionally crafted, cozy, and whimsical website. The site features the artwork predominantly but also highlights Peggy's life and her deep connection with her art. Incorporating flat artwork like sketches + 3D objects related to Peggy’s life (beach shells, sand, and rock paintings + sculptures) helps to strengthen the connection between Peggy and her artwork. The use of polaroid images also allows the user to experience the website as one would read a story and pushes the narrative aspect forward.
