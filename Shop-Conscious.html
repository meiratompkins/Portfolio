Original Hypothesis: We believe that creating a shopping platform for people who would like to know where to shop based on their style and personal preferences will allow the users to grow more accustomed to their own personal style and be able to find items they prefer at an increased rate. We will know the hypothesis is valid when we get more than 75% users to agree that they have found clothes/items that they like at a more increased rate and that they agree that they now have a more personal/cohesive style after using the platform/app/website.


Edited Hypothesis: We believe that creating a shopping app that focuses around ethical brands and educating shoppers on fast fashion for people who would like to know where to shop based on their style and personal preferences will allow users to become more educated on fast fashion.


Product I want to build: Because my hypothesis changed throughout this project, so did the actual product I was building.
